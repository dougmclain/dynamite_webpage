Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Dynamite Management ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect on dynamitemanagement.com, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this website you agree to the practices described below.

Information we collect

Information you provide. When you fill out our contact form we collect the name, email address, phone number (if provided), and message you submit so we can respond to your inquiry.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, our hosting provider and analytics tools (including Ahrefs Web Analytics) record standard log data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL, and timestamps. This information is used in aggregate to understand site traffic and improve the site.

Cookies. We use a small number of first-party cookies for site functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some site features may not work correctly if you do.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form.
  • To provide the financial management, accounting, and tax services you engage us for.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the website.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers that help us operate the site and our business (for example, email delivery, hosting, analytics), and only as needed to perform that work. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Data retention and security

We retain contact-form submissions and client records for as long as needed to provide services and meet recordkeeping requirements. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect the information we hold, but no system is perfectly secure.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by emailing info@hoafiscal.com. You may also opt out of non-essential analytics cookies through your browser.

Children's privacy

Our services are directed to homeowner associations and their boards, not children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above reflects the most recent update. Material changes will be posted on this page.

Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to info@hoafiscal.com or by mail to Dynamite Management.
